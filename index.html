Biography

I am a fourth year undergraduate student in Engineering Science, Robotics Major at the University of Toronto. I am currently working with Roger Grosse on machine learning for automated reasoning at the Vector Institute.

During my undergraduate study, I have worked on Lipschitz-constrained convolutional network with Roger Grosse and Jörn-Henrik Jacobsen. I also worked on unsupervised musical timbre transfer with Roger Grosse and Sageev Oore. I was a summer research student in Dynamic Systems Lab where I worked on machine learning for unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) control and distributed robot controls with Angela Schoellig.
